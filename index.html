FAQ Frequently Asked Questions

1. Who is this training for?

Our program is designed for anyone looking to build a career in virtual assistance, especially in administrative and real estate support. Whether you're a beginner or looking to refine your skills, Stemas Digital Hub is the perfect place to start.

2. How long is the training program?

The program lasts for one month, with live sessions held every Friday, Saturday and Sunday. You'll also gain access to recorded sessions and our supportive community. .

3. Do I need prior experience to enroll?

No prior experience is necessary! Our courses are tailored to meet the needs of beginners as well as those with some experience looking to upgrade their skills. .

4. What tools will I learn to use?

We’ll cover various tools, including Google Workspace, Canva, Upwork, LinkedIn, Payoneer, and Geegpay, along with project management and communication tools.

5. Do you provide jobs after the training?

No. We don't give you jobs after your training. We only equip you on the skills and show you how to apply for jobs. Applying for any job is 100% your responsibility.

6. Can I access the training materials after the program ends?

Absolutely! You’ll have access to recorded videos from our live sessions, so you can revisit lessons anytime you need a refresher.

7. What is included in the bonus classes?

Our bonus classes cover Canva design creation for professional visuals and step-by-step guidance for setting up Payoneer and Geegpay accounts to streamline payments from clients.

8. How do I join the Stemas Digital Hub community?

Make your payment to the account details on our payment process page and send the proof of payment to the WhatsApp number on the contact information.

Once we get your proof of payment, you’ll be added to the WhatsApp training class and granted lifetime access to our exclusive online community, where you can network with peers, share experiences, and access ongoing support.

9. How do I enroll in the program?

Simply click the "Enroll Now" button on our website, make you payment and send a proof of payment to secure your spot. You can also send a WhatsApp message for any clarification before you make your payment.

10. What support do you offer after the training?

We provide continuous support through our lifetime-access community, recorded lessons, and guidance as you navigate your virtual assistant career.
